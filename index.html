About Me

Hi, my name is Jason. I am a software engineer with a passion for building tools that empower the individual. Before pursuing a career in tech, I worked as an international English teacher, a yoga and meditation instructor, and a massage therapist. While the transition from education and wellness to tech might seem like a complete pivot, I see the fields as closely linked.

The North Star that guides me in my life and in my professional career is the question of empowerment. As a teacher and practitioner in the wellness space, I was interested in approaching the question of empowerment from the internal angle. How do the quality of our attention and the stories we tell ourselves empower or disempower us as individuals? And how can we better understand, cultivate, and harness the latent power of our attention? These questions drive my continuous self-inquiry practice.

The other approach to the question of empowerment is from the external angle. How do the tools and systems we use empower or disempower us as individuals? And how can we build and restructure these systems in such a way that fosters autonomy and freedom? Technology is central to these questions. As I see it, this field is the highest leverage place I can spend my time and attention.
